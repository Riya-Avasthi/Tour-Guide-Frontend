Must Visit Destinations

Visitors to Odisha should not miss experiencing the unique Rath Yatra in Puri, exploring the ancient caves of Udayagiri and Khandagiri, enjoying a boat ride on Chilika Lake, witnessing the traditional Odissi dance performance, and indulging in the vibrant tribal culture. Other must-try activities include savoring the local street food, visiting the traditional Odia handicraft markets, exploring the serene beaches, and trekking in the lush green hills.

Famous Cuisines

The culinary landscape of Odisha is diverse and offers five famous foods, including the scrumptious Dalma (lentils with vegetables), the flavorful Pakhala (fermented rice with curd), the mouth-watering Chhena Poda (cottage cheese dessert), the traditional Odia Pitha (rice cakes), and the delicious Machha Jhola (spicy fish curry).

Festivals

The state celebrates eight most famous festivals, including the grand Rath Yatra in Puri, Durga Puja, Diwali, Holi, Raja Parba, Konark Dance Festival, Puri Beach Festival, and the Odissi Music and Dance Festival.

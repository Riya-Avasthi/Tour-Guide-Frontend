Must Visit Destinations

Ten must-visit destinations include the picturesque Dzukou Valley, the enchanting Khonoma Village, the ancient Kachari Ruins, the vibrant Kisama Heritage Village, the majestic Japfu Peak, the serene Shilloi Lake, the mesmerizing Ntangki National Park, the cultural hub of Mokokchung, the tranquil Tuophema Village, and the iconic World War II Cemetery in Kohima.

Famous Cuisines

Nagaland boasts a distinct culinary tradition, with five famous foods that include the savory smoked pork curry, the fiery bamboo shoot pickle, the staple dish of fermented bamboo shoot with pork, the hearty Akhuni curry made from fermented soybeans, and the delicious smoked fish preparations.

Festivals

\The rich cultural heritage of Nagaland is celebrated through eight most famous festivals, including the Hornbill Festival, Moatsu Festival, Sekrenyi Festival, Aoling Festival, Tuluni Festival, Yemshe Festival, Nazu Festival, and Lui-Ngai-Ni Festival.

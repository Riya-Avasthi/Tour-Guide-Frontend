Must Visit Destinations

Kaziranga National Park: Famous for its population of Indian one-horned rhinoceroses, Kaziranga is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It's also home to a variety of other wildlife species, including tigers, elephants, and water buffalo.

Majuli Island: Majuli is the largest river island in the world and is located in the Brahmaputra River. It's known for its vibrant culture, Vaishnavite monasteries (Satras), and scenic beauty. The island offers a serene environment and is a great place to experience Assamese culture.

Guwahati: As the largest city in Assam, Guwahati serves as the gateway to the northeastern region of India. It's home to several attractions, including the Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Temple, Assam State Museum, and Assam State Zoo.

Famous Cuisines

Assamese Thali: A traditional Assamese meal typically consists of rice served with a variety of side dishes such as dal (lentils), fish curry, chicken curry, various vegetable preparations, and a selection of chutneys

Masor Tenga: This is a sour fish curry made with tomatoes, lemon, and sometimes flavored with various local herbs. It's a staple dish in Assamese cuisine.

Aloo Pitika: Mashed potatoes seasoned with mustard oil, onions, green chilies, and sometimes roasted or smoked fish. It's a simple yet flavorful dish often served as a side.

Festivals

Bihu Festival: Bihu is the most important festival of Assam, celebrated three times a year - Rongali Bihu (in April), Kongali Bihu (in October), and Bhogali Bihu (in January). It marks the Assamese New Year and is celebrated with dance, music, feasting, and cultural performances

Ambubachi Mela: Held at the Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati, this annual festival celebrates the menstruation of the goddess Kamakhya. It attracts thousands of devotees and tourists who come to seek blessings and witness various rituals and cultural events.

Magh Bihu: Also known as Bhogali Bihu, this festival marks the end of the harvesting season. It's celebrated with community feasts, bonfires, traditional games, and cultural performances.

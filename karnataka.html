Must Visit Destinations

Bangalore (Bengaluru): The capital city, known for its vibrant culture, gardens, nightlife, and tech industry.

Mysore (Mysuru): Famous for its majestic Mysore Palace, vibrant Dasara festival, and silk sarees.

Hampi: A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient ruins, temples, and stunning landscape

Chikmagalur: A picturesque hill station famous for its coffee estates, trekking spots, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Famous Cuisines

Udupi Cuisine: Renowned for its vegetarian dishes like dosas, idlis, vadas, and unique Udupi-style curries.

Coorg Cuisine: Influenced by Kodava traditions, includes dishes like pandi curry (pork curry), akki roti (rice flatbread), and bamboo shoot delicacies

Mangalorean Cuisine: Features a blend of coastal flavors, with dishes like Mangalorean fish curry, neer dosa, and kori rotti.

North Karnataka Cuisine: Known for its spicier and richer flavors, with dishes like jolada rotti (sorghum flatbread), enne gai (stuffed brinjal curry), and yennegai (stuffed eggplant curry).

Kodava Cuisine: Unique to the Coorg region, includes dishes like pandi curry (pork curry), kadambuttu (steamed rice balls), and koli curry (chicken curry).

Festivals

Dasara: Celebrated with great pomp and grandeur in Mysore, featuring processions, cultural performances, and the lighting of the Mysore Palace

Ugadi: The Kannada New Year, marked by special rituals, feasting, and cultural events

Ganesha Chaturthi: Celebrated with enthusiasm, particularly in Bangalore and coastal regions, with the installation of Ganesha idols, prayers, and community gatherings

Hampi Utsav: A cultural extravaganza held in Hampi, featuring music, dance, drama, and other traditional performances against the backdrop of Hampi's ruins.

Yugadi: The Kannada and Telugu New Year, celebrated with rituals, special dishes, and cultural programs.

Must Visit Destinations

The state is dotted with must-visit destinations, including the majestic Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai, the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Mahabalipuram, the tranquil hill station of Ooty, the historic Chola temples in Thanjavur, the pristine beaches of Kanyakumari, the spiritual town of Rameswaram, the picturesque Nilgiri Mountains, the wildlife sanctuary of Mudumalai, the colonial charm of Pondicherry, and the bustling city of Coimbatore.

Famous Cuisines

Tamil Nadu is a gastronomic delight with its unique and flavorful cuisine. Five famous foods include the aromatic Chettinad cuisine, the spicy Chidambaram brinjal gothsu, the delectable filter coffee, the crispy dosas, and the mouth-watering Madurai Jigarthanda.

Festivals

Tamil Nadu celebrates a plethora of festivals that reflect its rich cultural heritage. Eight most famous festivals include Pongal, Diwali, Navaratri, Karthigai Deepam, Chithirai Festival, Thaipusam, Panguni Uthiram, and Jallikattu.

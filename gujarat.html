Must Visit Destinations

Ahmedabad: Known for its rich history, bustling markets, and architectural marvels like the Sabarmati Ashram and the Sidi Saiyyed Mosque.

Gir National Park: Home to the Asiatic lions, it's a must-visit destination for wildlife enthusiasts.

Rann of Kutch: Famous for its white salt desert, especially during the Rann Utsav, a cultural festival showcasing the region's culture, crafts, and music.

Dwarka: An important pilgrimage site for Hindus, believed to be the ancient kingdom of Lord Krishna.

Famous Cuisines

Gujarati Thali: A platter of various dishes like dal, kadhi, roti, rice, vegetables, chutneys, and sweets like shrikhand and kadhi.

Dhokla: A steamed savory cake made from fermented rice and chickpea flour, often served as a snack or breakfast.

Fafda-Jalebi: A popular snack combination in Gujarat, especially during festivals like Dussehra

Undhiyu: A mixed vegetable curry cooked with spices, often served with puris during the festival of Uttarayan.

Festivals

Navratri: Celebrated with great enthusiasm across Gujarat with colorful garba dances and dandiya raas.

International Kite Festival (Uttarayan): Held annually in January in Ahmedabad and other cities, it's a vibrant celebration with thousands of kites filling the sky.

Modhera Dance Festival: Held in the backdrop of the Sun Temple at Modhera, showcasing classical dance forms.

Must Visit Destinations

Visitors to Sikkim can engage in numerous activities. Ten things to do or try include trekking in the Himalayas, visiting the flower-filled Fambong La Wildlife Sanctuary, experiencing the cable car ride in Gangtok, exploring the Banjhakri Falls, soaking in the medicinal waters of Yumthang Hot Springs, witnessing the annual Rhododendron Festival, embarking on the challenging Goecha La trek, enjoying river rafting in the Teesta River, tasting local cherry brandy, and visiting the Sikkim Himalayan Zoological Park.

Famous Cuisines

Food enthusiasts can savor the unique flavors of Sikkimese cuisine, with five famous foods being momo (dumplings), thukpa (noodle soup), gundruk (fermented leafy greens), phagshapa (pork with radish), and sel roti (rice-based doughnut).

Festivals

\The state celebrates a variety of festivals that showcase its cultural richness. Eight most famous festivals in Sikkim include Losar (Tibetan New Year), Dashain, Tihar, Losoong, Saga Dawa, Pang Lhabsol, Maghe Sankranti, and Namsoong.

Must Visit Destinations

Must-visit destinations include the iconic Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site showcasing Mughal architecture, and the majestic India Gate, a war memorial exuding grandeur. For a spiritual retreat, the Lotus Temple's unique lotus-shaped design and serene ambiance make it a must-see

Famous Cuisines

Delhi is complete without indulging in its culinary delights. From mouthwatering kebabs at Karim's to buttery parathas at Paranthe Wali Gali, the city's food scene is a gastronomic adventure waiting to be explored.

Festivals

Delhi celebrates a diverse array of festivals throughout the year. Diwali lights up the city with vibrant decorations and fireworks, while Holi brings joyous celebrations and colorful revelry. Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha are marked by prayers and feasting, Janmashtami sees devotees fasting and visiting temples, and Christmas brings a festive atmosphere with lights and carol singing.

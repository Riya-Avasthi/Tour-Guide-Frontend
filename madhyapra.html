Must Visit Destinations

The state is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Khajuraho with its exquisite temples, Sanchi known for its ancient Buddhist stupas, and Bhimbetka renowned for its prehistoric cave paintings. The scenic landscapes of Pachmarhi, the wildlife sanctuaries of Kanha and Bandhavgarh, the architectural marvels of Orchha, and the spiritual town of Ujjain are also essential stops for any traveler

Famous Cuisines

Food lovers can savor the culinary delights of Madhya Pradesh, including the popular poha, dal bafla, bhutte ka kees, Rogan Josh, and the delectable malpua

Festivals

Madhya Pradesh celebrates a variety of festivals, with the most famous ones being Diwali, Holi, Navratri, Dussehra, and the Khajuraho Dance Festival. The state's rich cultural heritage is evident in events like the Orchha Utsav and the Mandu Festival.

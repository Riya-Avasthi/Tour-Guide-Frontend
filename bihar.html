Must Visit Destinations

Bodh Gaya: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Buddhists, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

Nalanda: Home to the ancient Nalanda University, a renowned center of learning in ancient times and now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Patna: The capital city, with attractions like Patna Museum, Golghar, Mahatma Gandhi Setu, and the historic Patna Sahib Gurudwara.

Vaishali: An ancient city known for its association with Lord Buddha and as the birthplace of Lord Mahavira, the founder of Jainism

Rajgir: Famous for its hot springs, ancient ruins, and the Gridhakuta Hill, where Lord Buddha delivered many sermons

Famous Cuisines

Litti Chokha: A traditional Bihari dish consisting of roasted wheat balls (litti) served with mashed potato, brinjal, or tomato curry (chokha)

Sattu: A nutritious flour made from roasted gram, often used in dishes like sattu paratha, sattu drink, and sattu ka sharbat.

Chana Ghugni: A spicy and tangy preparation of black chickpeas, often served as a snack or side dish

Bihari Thali: A wholesome meal featuring dishes like dal pitha, aloo ka chokha, baingan bharta, and rice served with ghee

Khaja: A popular Bihari sweet made from refined flour, sugar, and ghee, often enjoyed during festivals and special occasions.

Festivals

Chhath Puja: A major festival celebrated with fervor in Bihar, dedicated to the worship of the Sun God, involving rituals like fasting, offering prayers, and taking dips in rivers

Sonepur Cattle Fair: Held in Sonepur, it is one of the largest cattle fairs in Asia, attracting traders and visitors from far and wide.

Pitrapaksha Mela: An annual fair held in Gaya, where people pay homage to their ancestors through rituals and offerings

Vishwa Shanti Stupa Festival: Celebrated at Rajgir, it marks the anniversary of the Vishwa Shanti Stupa with cultural programs, prayers, and meditation sessions.

Rajgir Mahotsav: A cultural festival held in Rajgir, showcasing the rich heritage and traditions of Bihar through music, dance, and art.

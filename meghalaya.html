Must Visit Destinations

Ten must-visit destinations in Meghalaya include the breathtaking Living Root Bridges of Cherrapunji, the serene Umiam Lake, the mystical Mawlynnong village (known as the cleanest village in Asia), the Nohkalikai Falls, the stunning Dawki River, the ancient Double Decker Living Root Bridge, the scenic Laitlum Canyons, the peaceful Shillong Peak, the picturesque Elephant Falls, and the sacred Mawphlang Sacred Forest.

Famous Cuisines

Food enthusiasts in Meghalaya can savor five famous dishes, including the traditional Jadoh (rice and meat dish), the spicy Doh Khleh (pork salad), the flavorful Pukhlein (rice cake), the aromatic Bamboo Shoot Curry, and the refreshing Kyat (local rice beer).

Festivals

Meghalaya celebrates a range of festivals that showcase its rich cultural diversity. Eight most famous festivals include Shad Suk Mynsiem, Wangala Festival, Behdienkhlam, Nongkrem Dance, Shillong Autumn Festival, Ka Pom-Blang Nongkrem, Christmas, and New Year's Eve celebrations.

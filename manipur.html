Must Visit Destinations

Must-visit destinations include the serene Loktak Lake, the historical Kangla Fort, the exotic Keibul Lamjao National Park, the enchanting Shri Govindjee Temple, the vibrant Khwairamband Bazaar, the mystical Sirohi National Park, and the picturesque INA Memorial Complex.

Famous Cuisines

Manipur boasts a distinct culinary identity, and five famous foods include the flavorful Eromba, the popular Kangshoi, the aromatic Morok Metpa, the sweet and savory Ooti, and the traditional rice-based bamboo shoot delicacy.

Festivals

Eight most famous festivals showcase Manipur's cultural vibrancy, including the Yaoshang Festival, Lai Haraoba, Ningol Chakouba, Cheiraoba, and the colorful Manipur Sangai Festival
